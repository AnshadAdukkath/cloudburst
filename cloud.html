About

The Cloudburst Prediction System utilizes cutting-edge technology to analyze meteorological parameters and weather patterns, providing valuable insights into the possibility of cloudbursts. Our system aims to enhance early warning capabilities and mitigate the impact of these natural disasters.

Notes about Cloudburst

A cloudburst is an extreme weather event characterized by intense rainfall over a small area and a short duration. These sudden and heavy downpours can lead to flash floods, landslides, and other hazardous conditions. Cloudbursts are often associated with thunderstorms and can cause significant damage to infrastructure and loss of life.
